## Interaction Notes

- **Hero video:** Autoplay, muted, loop. Pause on tab blur.
- **Broadcast cards:** Hover shows play overlay + yellow border glow
- **Calendar:** Click event → slide-up panel with details + watch link
- **LIVE indicator:** Pulse animation on nav item when streams active
- **Ticker:** Auto-scroll. Pause on hover.
- **Countdown:** JS real-time countdown to RACE WEEK 2026 (8 July)
- **Lazy loading:** All thumbnails and images
- **Smooth scroll:** Between anchor sections on single page
